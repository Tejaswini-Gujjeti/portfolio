About Me

I am Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, born October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, India. An aerospace scientist, I played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs, earning the title "Missile Man of India." Serving as India's President from 2002 to 2007, I focused on inspiring youth and advancing education. I passed away on July 27, 2015, leaving a legacy of scientific innovation and visionary leadership.
